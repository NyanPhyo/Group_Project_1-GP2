Our Team

A Snapshot of Group Nexus

Four students. Four different backgrounds. One shared vision. Group Nexus was formed at the start of Semester 1 with a single goal: deliver a world-class corporate website for the Technology Division of Three Aung One Wai.

Together, we bring expertise in front-end development, UI design, content strategy, quality assurance, and cybersecurity domain knowledge. Every page of this website reflects hours of collaboration, peer review, and creative problem-solving.

We meet three times a week — twice in class and once for a dedicated group working session every Thursday evening — to ensure the project stays on track and every team member's voice is heard.

Our Mission as a Team

Why We Build What We Build

Three Aung One Wai was founded on the belief that robust cybersecurity should not be the exclusive privilege of large corporations. Our Technology Division exists to bridge the gap — developing scalable, intelligent, and human-centred digital tools that empower businesses of every size to protect what matters most.

As the team tasked with representing this mission through the company's public-facing website, Group Nexus takes immense pride in every HTML element, every carefully chosen word, and every image that appears on these pages. This site is not just a university assignment — it is a living showcase of what we can collectively achieve.

"We don't just write code. We build trust — one secure, thoughtful, accessible line at a time."

— Group Nexus, Technology Division, Three Aung One Wai
